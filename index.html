About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Akademia G\u00f3rniczo-Hutnicza (AGH) University of Science and Technology, Krakow, Poland (Dec 2024-Present), specializing in Discrete Element Method (DEM) simulations. My research focuses on numerical analysis of lunar soil excavation processes and mechanical behavior using DEM under microgravity conditions.

I completed my Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee with a CGPA of 8.4/10.0 (Advisor: Prof. Anshu Anand), where I developed expertise in numerical simulations, experimental validation, and computational modeling of granular systems, particularly focusing on segregation phenomena in rotating drums and vibrated packed beds with nonspherical particles.

  • Research Focus: Lunar regolith mechanics, DEM simulations, granular mixing & segregation, particle technology, nonspherical particle dynamics
  • Expertise: Discrete Element Method (DEM), LIGGGHTS, Rocky-DEM, EDEM, Python, Machine Learning, Experimental validation, Parameter calibration
  • Awards: Finalist (Top 8) for LKMT Award for best Ph.D. thesis; George Klinzing Best PhD Award nominee; DAAD scholarship recipient
  • Publications: 11 Q1 journal articles + 2 manuscripts under review

Looking forward to pursuing a faculty position at leading Indian research institutes (IIT/NIT) where I can combine expertise in DEM simulations with teaching and mentoring the next generation of researchers.
