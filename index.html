About Me

Career Summary

Postdoctoral Fellow and Computational Scientist at AGH University of Science and Technology, Krakow, Poland. My work integrates advanced Discrete Element Method (DEM) simulations with experimental techniques to understand granular materials—especially lunar regolith and planetary excavation. Recognized for research presentations in Germany (DEM9, PARTEC), independent research grants, and several best presentation and poster awards. Nominated for Lovraj Kumar Memorial Trust Best PhD Thesis and George Klinzing Best PhD in Particle Technology.

Expertise

DEM Modeling (LIGGGHTS, EDEM, Rocky), multi-equipment experiments (drums/beds), parameter calibration, ML-integrated modeling, Python, and grant writing.
