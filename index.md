### About

Explorer, mentor, and philomath working across **Discrete Element Method (DEM)**, granular materials, and computational modeling with strong emphasis on mixing/segregation and data-driven methods.

- **Postdoctoral Fellow, AGH Univ. of Krakow:** Numerical analysis of lunar regolith excavation with DEM (microgravity, validation via repose and shear tests).
- **Ph.D., Chemical Engineering (IIT Roorkee):** Segregation in rotating drums for nonspherical particles; 11 Q1 papers, multiple awards and nominations.

---

### Expertise

DEM (EDEM, Rocky-DEM, LIGGGHTS), Python, mixing/segregation, HPC, Paraview, ImageJ, ML for particle systems.
