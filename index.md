## About

Explorer, mentor, and philomath working across **Discrete Element Method (DEM)**, granular materials, and computational modeling with strong emphasis on **particle segregation, mixing dynamics, and data-driven methods**. Experienced in **numerical simulations**, **experimental research**, and **machine learning** applications to complex particle systems.

- **Ph.D., Chemical Engineering, IIT Roorkee** (2018–2024): Segregation in rotating drums for nonspherical particles; **11 Q1 publications**; nominee for prestigious awards
