## Current Research Areas

### 1. Lunar Regolith Excavation Mechanics
**Postdoctoral Research at AGH Univ. of Krakow (2024–Present)**

Numerical modeling of lunar soil excavation under microgravity conditions using Discrete Element Method (DEM).

**Key Focus:**
- Microgravity effects on particle dynamics
- Model validation against experimental data
- Repose angle and shear strength measurements
- Comparison of terrestrial vs. lunar regolith behavior

**Tools:** EDEM, Rocky-DEM, LIGGGHTS, Python, Paraview

---

### 2. Particle Segregation in Rotating Systems
**Ph.D. Research (IIT Roorkee, 2018–2024)**

Experimental and numerical investigation of segregation phenomena in rotating drums for non-spherical particles.

**Key Contributions:**
- 11 Q1 journal publications
- Developed experimental rigs for radial/axial drums
- DEM simulations with particle shape effects
- Machine learning pipelines for segregation prediction
- Multiple conference presentations and awards

---

### 3. Particle Breakage in Vibro-Fluidized Beds
**Postdoctoral Research at IIT Bombay (2024)**

Investigation of breakage mechanisms for non-spherical particles under vibration.

**Status:** Preliminary DEM results and experimental validation in progress
