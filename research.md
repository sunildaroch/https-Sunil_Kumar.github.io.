## Current & Past Research Positions

### 1. Postdoctoral Fellow - AGH University of Science and Technology, Krakow, Poland
**December 2024 – Present**

**Department:** Robotics & Mechatronics, Faculty of Mechanical Engineering & Robotics

**Research Focus:**
- **Lunar Soil Excavation Mechanics** under microgravity conditions using Discrete Element Method (DEM)
- Numerical analysis of lunar regolith excavation processes and mechanical behavior
- DEM model development and calibration for lunar excavation systems
- Validation against experimental data including repose angle and shear tests
- Comparison of terrestrial vs. lunar regolith behavior

**Key Activities:**
- Designing and executing numerical simulations for lunar regolith excavation
- Model validation using experimental data from specialized test equipment
- Collaboration with international research teams on lunar exploration projects
